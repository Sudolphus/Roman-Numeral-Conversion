Helpful tips for Roman Numerals!

  • M = 1000
  • D = 500
  • C = 100
  • L = 50
  • X = 10
  • V = 5
  • I = 1
  • Roman Numerals are equal to the sum of their values; if a numeral is followed by a numeral of greater value, it becomes negative in value.
